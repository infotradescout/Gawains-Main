# AutoBott

Repo: `infotradescout/AutoBott`

## Purpose

AutoBott is trading/sports decision support with risk controls and real validation.

## Doctrine

- Phase 1 only unless explicitly expanded.
- Read-only decision cards.
- No live orders.
- No broker execution.
- No fake runtime data.
- Deterministic fixtures are acceptable for tests.
- Risk controls required.
- Paper validation before live claims.
